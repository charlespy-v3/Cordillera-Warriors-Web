About

Cordillera Warriors Taekwondo is a family-oriented team dedicated to developing discipline, confidence, self-defense, fitness, and sportsmanship through Taekwondo training. For almost 5 years, our team has welcomed students from different schools and backgrounds who share the passion to learn and grow together. We believe Taekwondo is not only about competition, but also about building character, respect, perseverance, and strong friendships. Everyone is welcome, from beginners to experienced athletes. We provide affordable quality training while maintaining a friendly and supportive family-like environment.
